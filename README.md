# Silhouette-Sidebar侧影-侧边栏
一款侧边一款轻量高效的 Android 悬浮侧边栏app，支持快速启动应用与常用操作，提升多任务使用效率。缓解小米/红米平板和原生安卓系统（如pixel手机）没有侧边栏的痛点

在日常使用手机时，我们经常需要在多个应用之间来回切换，或者重复进入桌面、应用列表、系统设置页面才能完成某些操作。侧影-侧边栏通过常驻屏幕边缘的悬浮入口，将这些高频操作集中到一个可以随时呼出的侧边栏中，让用户在当前界面不被打断的情况下快速完成操作。
无论是在聊天、浏览网页、阅读文档、看视频，还是进行多任务处理时，用户都可以通过屏幕边缘轻滑或点击的方式呼出侧边栏，快速打开常用应用或执行预设操作，从而减少重复点击，提高手机操作效率。

它的设计目标是：
- 让常用应用触手可及
- 让高频操作更快完成
- 让多任务切换更加顺手
- 让悬浮工具保持轻量、克制、不打扰
- 让不同用户可以根据自己的习惯配置侧边栏

## 核心功能：

### 悬浮侧边栏

应用提供一个常驻屏幕边缘的悬浮入口，用户可以在任意界面快速呼出侧边栏。

侧边栏不依赖桌面环境，即使用户正在使用其他应用，也可以随时打开快捷面板，减少返回桌面或进入应用列表的操作成本。

### 快速启动应用

用户可以将常用应用添加到侧边栏中，例如微信、浏览器、相册、文件管理器、音乐播放器、地图等。

当需要切换应用时，只需呼出侧边栏并点击对应图标，即可快速打开目标应用，适合频繁多任务切换的使用场景。


### 轻量化交互体验

侧影-侧边栏强调简洁、快速和低打扰。

侧边栏入口常驻屏幕边缘，但不会占用过多显示空间；需要使用时可以快速呼出，不需要时则保持安静存在，尽量减少对当前应用内容的遮挡。

"Side Shadow" is a lightweight and efficient Android floating sidebar app designed to facilitate quick app launches and common actions, thereby boosting multitasking efficiency. It addresses a common pain point for users of Xiaomi/Redmi tablets and devices running stock Android (such as Pixel phones), which often lack a built-in sidebar feature.

In our daily mobile usage, we frequently find ourselves switching back and forth between multiple apps, or repeatedly navigating to the home screen, app drawer, or system settings to complete specific tasks. "Side Shadow" streamlines these high-frequency actions by consolidating them into a single, easily accessible sidebar—summoned via a floating trigger anchored to the screen's edge. This allows users to perform tasks quickly and seamlessly without interrupting their current workflow.

Whether you are chatting, browsing the web, reading documents, watching videos, or actively multitasking, you can simply swipe or tap the screen's edge to summon the sidebar. This enables you to quickly launch favorite apps or execute preset actions, thereby minimizing repetitive taps and significantly enhancing your overall mobile operating efficiency.

Its core design objectives are:
- To keep your most-used apps within easy reach.
- To accelerate the completion of high-frequency tasks.
- To make multitasking switches feel smoother and more intuitive.
- To ensure the floating tool remains lightweight, unobtrusive, and non-intrusive.
- To allow individual users to customize the sidebar to suit their own unique habits and preferences.

## Core Features:

### Floating Sidebar

The app provides a persistent floating trigger anchored to the edge of the screen, allowing users to quickly summon the sidebar from *any* interface.

The sidebar operates independently of the home screen environment; even when you are actively using another application, you can instantly open the shortcut panel without needing to return to the home screen or navigate through the app drawer—thereby reducing the operational overhead associated with switching contexts.

### Quick App Launch

Users can add their most frequently used applications to the sidebar—such as messaging apps (e.g., WeChat), web browsers, photo galleries, file managers, music players, navigation apps, and more.

When you need to switch applications, simply summon the sidebar and tap the corresponding icon to instantly launch your target app—making it an ideal solution for scenarios involving frequent multitasking.


### Lightweight & Intuitive Interaction

"Side Shadow" prioritizes simplicity, speed, and minimal intrusion.

While the sidebar trigger remains anchored to the screen's edge, it occupies very little screen real estate. It can be quickly summoned whenever needed, yet remains unobtrusive and "quiet" when not in use—designed to minimize any obstruction of the content currently displayed within your active application. ## Key Features

- Supports quick access to a floating sidebar by swiping from the screen edge
- Allows adding shortcuts for frequently used applications
- Enables rapid launching of target apps while using other applications
- Supports customized shortcut sets tailored to different usage scenarios
- Lightweight interaction design that does not interfere with the currently active application
- Streamlined workflow with minimal steps, ideal for frequent use
- Optimized for multitasking, one-handed operation, and productivity enhancement
- Clean, minimalist interface with a low barrier to entry
